Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my partnership benefits?

All partnership benefits are automatically activated upon payment confirmation. You can access them through your partner dashboard. For specific benefits like booth setup or speaking sessions, our partnership team will contact you directly.

How can I upgrade my partnership level?

You can upgrade your partnership by visiting the Partnership tab in your dashboard and selecting the "Upgrade Partnership" option. Our team will contact you to complete the process.

Where can I find my payment receipts?

All payment receipts and invoices are available in the Resources tab under the Documents section. These documents are also visible to the Super Admin for verification purposes.

How do I submit my schedule to the admin?

After selecting events to attend in the Agenda tab, click "Submit Schedule to Admin" in your schedule widget. This will notify the admin of your planned attendance.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience any technical difficulties with the dashboard, please contact our support team using the Contact Support option. Include details about the issue and any error messages you received.
