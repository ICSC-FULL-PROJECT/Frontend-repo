Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my presentation materials?
You can upload your presentation materials through the "Upload Materials" tab in your dashboard. Supported formats include PowerPoint (PPTX), PDF, and Keynote. The maximum file size is 100MB.
What are the technical requirements for my presentation?
We recommend using 16:9 aspect ratio for slides. The venue will provide projectors, microphones, and clickers. For detailed technical specifications, please check the "AV Specifications" document in the Resources section.
Can I make changes to my session after registration?
Minor changes to your presentation title or description can be made up to 2 weeks before the event. For major changes or if you need to cancel your participation, please contact the speaker coordinator as soon as possible.
How do I access the speaker preparation session?
The speaker preparation session will be conducted virtually via Zoom. The link will be sent to your registered email address 48 hours before the session. Attendance is mandatory for all speakers.
What expenses are covered for speakers?
Diamond and Gold package speakers receive complimentary conference registration and accommodation for 2 nights. Silver and Bronze package speakers receive discounted registration. Travel expenses are the responsibility of the speaker unless otherwise arranged.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble accessing the speaker portal
Please try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try using a different browser. If the problem persists, contact our technical support team at techsupport@icsc2026.gov.ng
My uploaded files aren't appearing in the portal
File uploads may take a few minutes to process depending on file size and internet connection. If your files don't appear after 10 minutes, please try uploading again or contact technical support.
