ABOUT THE EVENT

The International Civil Service Conference is a prestigious gathering of policymakers, government officials, and stakeholders aimed at discussing innovations and best practices in public administration.

Scheduled to take place in Abuja, Nigeria, the conference will focus on reforms, digital transformation, and capacity building within the civil service. Key speakers from Nigeria and across the globe will share insights on enhancing efficiency and governance.

Participants will engage in workshops, panel discussions, and networking sessions to drive impactful policy changes. The event provides a platform for knowledge exchange fostering collaboration between the public and private sectors.

Registrations are now open, and we welcome partnerships to help drive the success of this transformative initiative.

Conference Stages

Transform

Driving fundamental reforms in structures, policies, and leadership approaches to reshape public institutions for modern governance.

Rejuvenate

Revitalising the public service workforce through skills renewal, youth inclusion, wellbeing, and a culture that energises performance.

Innovate

Encouraging experimentation, digital adoption, and new service models that solve public problems in smarter and more efficient ways.

Accelerate

Speeding up policy execution and service delivery by removing bottlenecks, simplifying processes, and improving cross-agency collaboration.

Accomplish

Focusing on measurable outcomes, performance accountability, and delivering tangible results that improve citizens lives.

Impact

Using data, evidence, and feedback to ensure public policies and programmes create lasting social and economic value.

Aspire

Setting bold national and institutional visions, benchmarking globally, and positioning the civil service for future excellence.

Activate

Turning strategies into action through leadership commitment, workforce mobilisation, and secure digital systems that support execution.
