2026 Agenda

Examining and inspiring innovation in Policy Development, Procurement, Processes Automation, Data, Workspace, Service Design and Delivery, Fraud across central government.

WEDNESDAY 25 June

All times quoted are West Africa Time (WAT).

Main stages | Roundtables

Main Stages

Bringing together public service leaders from around Nigeria, the International Civil Service Conference explored how public servants can face new challenges through rejuvenating, innovating and accelerating public services.

Time Session
09:00 - 09:45
Opening Keynote: The Future of Public Service
Dr. Adebayo Williams, Minister of Public Service
Setting the stage for the conference with insights on the evolving role of public service in a rapidly changing world.
09:45 - 10:30
Digital Transformation in Government
Prof. Chinedu Okoro, Director of Digital Services
Exploring how digital technologies are reshaping public service delivery and citizen engagement.
10:30 - 11:00
Coffee Break & Networking
An opportunity to connect with fellow attendees and speakers.
11:00 - 12:00
Panel: Building Resilient Public Institutions
Moderated by Dr. Fatima Bello with representatives from UNDP, World Bank, and African Development Bank
A discussion on strategies for strengthening public institutions to withstand future challenges.
Time Session
13:00 - 13:45
Rejuvenating Public Service Workforce
Mrs. Grace Okafor, Head of Civil Service
Strategies for attracting, developing, and retaining talent in the public sector.
13:45 - 14:30
Mental Health and Wellbeing in Public Service
Dr. Tunde Adeyemi, Clinical Psychologist
Addressing burnout and promoting mental wellness among public servants.
Time Session
15:00 - 15:45
Innovation Labs in Government
Mr. Segun Adewale, Innovation Director
How innovation labs are driving change and solving complex problems in public service.
15:45 - 16:30
Case Studies: Successful Public Service Innovations
Various presenters from different ministries
Real-world examples of innovation that have improved service delivery and citizen satisfaction.

Roundtables

Interactive discussions on specific topics led by subject matter experts.

Digital Service Delivery

14:00 - 15:30

Policy Innovation

Target Audience: Policy Makers, Researchers
16:00 - 17:30

Data-Driven Governance

Led by: Ms. Amina Jibril

How data analytics and evidence-based approaches are transforming public administration.

Target Audience: Data Scientists, Program Managers
