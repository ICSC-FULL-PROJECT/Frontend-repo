About the Event

Discover what makes the International Civil Service Conference a transformative experience

The International Civil Service Conference 2026 is a premier gathering of public service leaders, policymakers, and stakeholders from around the globe. Hosted by the Federal Government of Nigeria, this landmark event brings together the brightest minds in public administration to explore innovations, share best practices, and drive meaningful transformation in government services.

Scheduled to take place at Eagle Square in Abuja, Nigeria, the conference will focus on critical reforms, digital transformation, and capacity building within the civil service. Distinguished speakers from Nigeria and across the world will share insights on enhancing efficiency, governance, and public service delivery.

Participants will engage in dynamic workshops, thought-provoking panel discussions, and valuable networking sessions designed to drive impactful policy changes. The event provides a unique platform for knowledge exchange, fostering collaboration between the public and private sectors to address the most pressing challenges facing governments today.

Key Focus Areas

  • Developing strong leadership with mutual respect between ministers and senior officials
  • Building a highly skilled, inclusive public sector workforce ready for future challenges
  • Fostering an agile, digital, and innovative culture focused on delivery
  • Implementing service delivery structures that transcend organizational silos
  • Building government trusted by citizens through transparency and accountability
  • Leveraging technology to enhance service delivery and citizen engagement

Through keynote speeches, expert panels, and closed-door roundtables, the conference will help civil services worldwide make progress in key priority areas including capability building, talent management, performance management, innovation, digitalization, and staff welfare.
