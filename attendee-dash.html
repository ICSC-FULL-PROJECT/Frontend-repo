Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I watch the live stream?

Go to the "Live Stream" tab and click "Play Live Stream". The conference will be streamed live during scheduled hours.

Can I watch recorded sessions?

Yes! All recorded sessions are available on our YouTube channel. Click the "YouTube Channel" button to access all videos.

How do I update my schedule?

You can update your schedule by clicking the "Attend This Day" button on any day in the Dashboard tab.

How do I contact support?
