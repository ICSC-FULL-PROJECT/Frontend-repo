VENUE

The inaugural International Civil Service Conference will take place in Eagle Square, Abuja, Nigeria. Join us to see Eagle Square as never before.

25-26 June 2026

Eagle Square

The space will be brought to life to welcome civil servants from across Nigeria and around the world to discuss how to rejuvenate, innovate and accelerate the public service.

The square will accommodate a major theatre, many breakout theatres, a food court and expansive exhibition, set across three halls.

Important: Your official government ID must be presented upon entry if you are attending in-person.

Visiting Eagle Square, Abuja

Eagle Square is located between the Head of Service and the Federal Secretariat buildings in the Central Business District of Abuja.

The venue has a large space for parking, and a bus stop just outside the main entrance making it easily accessible.

Address:

Shehu Shagari Way, Three Arms Zone, Abuja, Nigeria
